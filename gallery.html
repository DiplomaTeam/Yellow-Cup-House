Check out our review on Trip Advisor:

I pass this place on my way to work, they're doing €2 coffee (in the morning at any rate) right now & it's definitely worth it, they have their loyalty cards as well - first free coffee this morning :)
The staff are very friendly and both make a yummy mocha - can't recall their names, sorry guys!
I've been enjoying their "No. 5" sandwich, €5 for roasted veg & buffalo mozzarella on ciabatta... yes please!
Tried breakfast here one morning - I had an omelette which worked out quite nice, my companion had the full Irish breakfast - the eggs were a tiny bit underdone, that said, we were there at 8.10 am & the first breakfasters of the day.
